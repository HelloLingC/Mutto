Contribute translation for Mutto!

2021-07-16

It’s welcome to contribute translation to your own country!

Mutto and me thanks you a lot!

Whether it’s in need

First of all, you need to check out this project on GitHub, to see whether you need add or compelete your country’s language.

After your confirming, you can start to add your language with this post!
